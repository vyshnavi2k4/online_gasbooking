Do you need to submit KYC?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is required for identity verification when applying for a new LPG connection, transferring ownership, or for certain subsidy checks.

Typical Documents

  • Identity: Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport / PAN
  • Address proof: Aadhaar / Utility bill / Ration card / Passport
  • Passport-size photo (if requested)
